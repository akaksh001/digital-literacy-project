## 📌 Project Overview

This repository contains my Digital Literacy Project, completed as part of the CSE0001 course. The project is designed to help students understand the importance of digital skills in today’s world. As a Student Digital Ambassador, I explored various tools, platforms, and practices that are essential for effective and responsible use of technology.

The project consists of five tasks that cover key areas such as digital awareness, professional online presence, coding platforms, communication skills, and cyber safety. Each task helped me develop practical knowledge that can be applied in both academic and real-life situations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* To understand the concept of digital literacy
* To explore useful digital tools for students
* To build a professional online presence
* To learn proper email communication
* To understand cybercrime and online safety

## 📝 Task Details

### 🎨 Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

In this task, I created an infographic to explain the concept of digital literacy and its importance. The design includes topics such as digital tools, safe internet practices, and the role of digital skills in education. This task helped me understand how to present information visually in a clear and engaging manner.

---

### 👨‍💻 Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio

I created and updated my profiles on professional platforms. This included setting up a GitHub profile, creating a LinkedIn account, and exploring a coding platform. This task highlighted the importance of maintaining a professional digital identity and how it can support future career opportunities.

---

### 💻 Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms

For this task, I solved a beginner-level coding problem and created a digital literacy quiz using an online form tool. I also analyzed responses using a spreadsheet. This helped me understand how technology can be used for learning, assessment, and collaboration.

### 📧 Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Communication

I drafted two professional emails: one for requesting an assignment extension and another for applying for an internship. Additionally, I created a checklist of do’s and don’ts for responsible social media use. This task improved my understanding of formal communication in digital environments.



---

### 🔐 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

In this task, I studied phishing as a common cybercrime and created a prevention checklist. I learned about the risks associated with online activities and the importance of safe digital practices. This task increased my awareness of cybersecurity and responsible internet usage.
