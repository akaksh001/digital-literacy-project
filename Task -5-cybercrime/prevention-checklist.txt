Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

1. Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone.
2. Always verify the sender before clicking on links.
3. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
4. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
6. Always check website URLs before entering personal information.
7. Do not download files from unknown sources.

UPI Safety Tips:
8. Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps.
9. Do not scan QR codes from unknown sources.

Reporting:
If you face cyber fraud, report it immediately at https://cybercrime.gov.in or call helpline number 1930.
