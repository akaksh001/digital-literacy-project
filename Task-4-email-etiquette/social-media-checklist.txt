## 5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student. 

### 5 DO's ✅
✔ Maintain a professional profile  
✔ Think before posting  
✔ Use privacy settings  
✔ Share useful and positive content  
✔ Respect others online  


### 5 DON'TS ❌
✖ Do not share personal information publicly  
✖ Avoid posting offensive content  
✖ Do not engage in cyberbullying  
✖ Avoid clicking unknown links  
✖ Do not spread misinformation
